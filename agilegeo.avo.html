agilegeo.avo.zoeppritz(vp1, vs1, rho1, vp0, vs0, rho0, theta1)[source]¶

Full Zoeppritz solution, considered the definitive solution. Calculates the angle dependent p-wave reflectivity of an interface between two mediums.

Parameters:
  • vp1 – The p-wave velocity of the upper medium.
  • vs1 – The s-wave velocity of the upper medium.
  • rho1 – The density of the upper medium.
  • vp0 – The p-wave velocity of the lower medium.
  • vs0 – The s-wave velocity of the lower medium.
  • rho0 – The density of the lower medium.
  • theta1 – An array of incident angles to use for reflectivity calculation [degrees].
Returns:

a vector of len(theta1) containing the reflectivity value corresponding to each angle.

agilegeo.avo.time_to_depth(data, vmodel, dt, dz, twt=True, mode='nearest')[source]¶

Converts data from the time domain to the depth domain given a velocity model.

Parameters:
  • data – The data to convert, will work with a 1 or 2D numpy numpy array. array(samples,traces).
  • vmodel – P-wave velocity model that corresponds to the data. Must be the same shape as data.
  • dt – The sample interval of the input data [s], or an array of times.
  • dz – The sample interval of the output data [m], or an array of depths
  • twt – Use twt travel time, defaults to true
Returns:

The data resampled in the depth domain.

agilegeo.avo.avseth_fluidsub(vp, vs, rho, phi, rhof1, rhof2, kmin, kf1, kf2)[source]¶

Naive fluid substitution from Avseth et al. No pressure/temperature correction.

Parameters:
  • vp – P-wave velocity
  • vs – S-wave velocity
  • rho – bulk density
  • phi – porosity (i.e. 0.20)
  • rhof1 – bulk density of original fluid (base case)
  • rhof2 – bulk density of substitute fluid (subbed case)
  • kmin – bulk modulus of solid mineral(s)
  • kf1 – bulk modulus of original fluid
  • kf2 – bulk modulus of substitue fluid

Only works for SI units right now.

Returns Vp, Vs, and rho for the substituted case

agilegeo.avo.smith_fluidsub(vp, vs, rho, phi, rhow, rhohc, sw, swnew, kw, khc, kclay, kqtz, vclay=None, rhownew=None, rhohcnew=None, kwnew=None, khcnew=None)[source]¶

Naive fluid substitution from Smith et al. 2003 No pressure/temperature correction.

Parameters:
  • vp – P-wave velocity
  • vs – S-wave velocity
  • rho – bulk density
  • rhow – density of water
  • rhohc – density of HC
  • rhownew – density of water in subbed case (optional)
  • rhohcnew – density of HC in subbed case (optional)
  • phi – porosity (fraction)
  • sw – water saturation in base case
  • swnew – water saturation in subbed case
  • kw – bulk modulus of water
  • khc – bulk modulus of hydrocarbon
  • kwnew – bulk modulus of water in subbed case (optional)
  • khcnew – bulk modulus of hydrocarbon in subbed case (optional)
  • vclay – Vclay (give this or vsh)
  • vsh – Vsh (or give vclay; vclay = 0.7 * vsh)
  • kclay – bulk modulus of clay (DEFAULT?)
  • kqtz – bulk modulus of quartz (DEFAULT?)

Only works for SI units right now.

Returns Vp, Vs, and rho for the substituted case.
